Watch Party

This app, developed by me and a team of four other people caters to movie lovers. Watch party makes it easier for movie lovers to pick their next movie. Using the youtube and OMDB APIs we were able to create a single page with all the info you need to help pick your next movie. The OMDB API allows us to pull various types of information regaring any movie. Things such as runtime, cast, and descriptions all the way to movie reviews from the most credible movie reviewers. By combining this with the youtube API users can read about a movies description and reviews and watch the trailer, all with one quick search.

JabberyWocky

This app is catered to book worms who love share their reads with others. JabberWocky is a social media platform for books lovers. Users can sign up and create an account. By creating an account users are offered a variety of features. They can explore the home page, which consists of other users latest reviews on books. They can also interact with these reviews by leaving comments. On their profile page they can create a reading list. On their profile page they will also find the reviews they posted. They can also leave their own reviewer by leaving a comment and a star rating. If they are unsure about a certain book they can utilize the search bar to see if any has left a reviewer on a particular book.
